ACM-W REGIONAL CELEBRATION 2021

ACM-W Regional Celebration 2021 is jointly organised by L.D. College of Engineering, on 4th December 2021.

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field. The highlight of this year’s event will be an expert talk by women technocrats.

The theme chosen for ACM-W Regional Celebration 2021 is “Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security for Enhancing Digital Life”.
