ACM-W REGIONAL CELEBRATION 2021

ACM-W Regional Celebration 2021 is jointly organised by L.D. College of Engineering, on 4th December 2021.

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field. The highlight of this year’s event will be a panel discussion by eminent women technocrats, on the topic “Does Gender Impact Technology?”
The theme chosen for ACM-W Regional Celebration 2021 is “Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security for Enhancing Digital Life”.
